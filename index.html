About ABODUNRIN TEMITOPE EMMANUEL

I am a student of Cloud Engineering at AltSchool Africa with the Student ID: ALT/SOE/024/0242. I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. My interest in Cloud Engineering began just after the completion of my National Youth Service Year in 2023. I learned about AltSchool Africa as one of the best online schools in Africa with the aid to help young africans to develop technical skills which are globally accepted.

I enrolled into AltSchool of Engineering with the aim to pursue a career in Cloud Engineering, develop as a full-time Cloud Engineer, explore and grow in the field, gain knowledge and skills that will enable me become a successful Cloud Engineer, connect with people and create a long lasting and beneficial friendship.
